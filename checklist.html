Before the Semester

  • Review your class schedule and locate buildings
  • Set up your study space with essential supplies
  • Research campus resources (tutoring, health services)

First Week Success

  • Review all syllabi and note assignment deadlines
  • Introduce yourself to professors during office hours
  • Set up your study schedule with dedicated time blocks
  • Join at least one student organization or club
