QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the College Confident 101 program.

How long do I have access to the training?

You'll have lifetime access to all course materials and any future updates we make to the program. Learn at your own pace and revisit modules whenever you need a refresher.

Is this only for incoming freshmen?

Not at all! While the program is excellent for new students, we have many sophomores, juniors, and even seniors who benefit from the training. It's never too late to improve your college skills and mindset.

How is this different from what my college orientation provides?

College orientations typically cover basic logistics and campus information. Our program dives deep into the practical skills, mindsets, and strategies for academic success that most orientations simply don't have time to address.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day, no-questions-asked money-back guarantee. If you don't find the program valuable, simply email us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

How much time does the training take to complete?

The core program can be completed in about 8-10 hours. However, we recommend spreading it out over 2-4 weeks to allow time to implement the strategies and reflect on your progress.
